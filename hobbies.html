Hobbies

I enjoy a lot of hobbies. It gives me something different to do depending on my mood, the weather, or time of year (knitting in the summer is really hot!). But there is one hobby I constantly go back to no matter what season: sewing. I am a proud owner of 4 sewing machines! Well, 3 sewing machines and 1 serger to be exact. It's a lot but I love them. I haven't gotten around to giving them names but at some point it might just happen.
